About Joshua Omoga

Joshua Omoga is a Cultural Orator, Public Speaker, a Minister of the word.

He utilizes humour, storytelling, and simple life lessons to excite thinking and boost enthusiasm and create inspiration.

Joshua is the Chief Executive Officer at True Host Bureau.

Man did not just come to be, there must have been a source and a reason. Knowing that each person is differently gifted to live and thrive here on earth, I use the story of man through space and time to break negative thought patterns, drive norm-change and inspire individuals to consciously realize their full potential with dignity
