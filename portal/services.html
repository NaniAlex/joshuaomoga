Services I Provide

Public Speaking

Whether it is Change, growth or performance in individual and groups, one has to be convicted that it is worth taking the challenge to make it happen. Life often has distractors that make even he strongest hearts faint. We speak inspiration into people so that they live each moment of life with zeal. In the end they succeed in just whatever they set their minds to achieve. A group succeeds because of individuals in it succeeded.

Joshua delivers Key note speeches and Inspiration talks to groups, organizations, corporate companies and church congregations.

Story Telling

As a cultural orator, Joshua becomes older than his age by unearthing the origins of things and people and drives audiences to make informed choices

He specialises in the story of the Luo; their origin and migration into present places across Africa. What made them distinct and what has been carried into modern lives and any forgotten values worth bringing back. His question often is In Jaluo, to in Jaluo nade?




Creative Bible Teaching

Joshua is a Church minister and a practicing Evangelist with a passion for Teaching. He uses Bible lessons to teach audiences in and outside the church.
